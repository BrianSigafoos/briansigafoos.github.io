Crash course on Dubai’s startup ecosystem

Times have changed from 6 years ago (2008) when our startup DUPLAYS was just getting off the ground, holding meetings at Caribou Coffee. Today Dubai has a number of co-working spaces, accelerators and an overall much more vibrant startup scene.

Co-working spaces

  • The Shelter - co-working, events, and more
  • MAKE Business Hub - Dubai Marina cafe, co-working space
  • Impact Hub - Dubai - part of network of 30+ “hubs” around the world
  • The Cribb - part of Innovation 360 that also run i360 and Turn8 below
  • The Pavilion - sadly closing/closed
  • Astrolabs - coming soon!

Startup news

  • Wamda - the source for MENA startup news, that also has a fund, and does great events like Mix N Mentor

Accelerators

  • i360 Accelerator
  • Turn8
  • In5
  • Seed Startup
  • Sicilon Oasis
  • Afkar.me

Startups

  • Dubizzle - Buy, sell, find anything in Dubai
  • DUPLAYS - Play rec sports and meet new people
  • PropertyFinder.ae - Rent/buy your apartment
  • Nabbesh - Post and search for skills, find a co-founder
  • Eureeca - Crowd investing platform
  • Restronaut - Social dining, like “DUPLAYS for dining out” :)
  • Careem - Dubai’s own Uber / car service
  • Qordoba - Translation marketplace
  • Astrolabs - Startup education programs “Scaling online startups”
  • FishFishMe - Go fishing!
  • JadoPado - Buy electronics and more online
  • …plus 100’s more on AngelList angel.co/dubai
