Hi, I’m Brian

I’m a software engineer and startup founder. I love building products that try to make the world better and bring people together.

Since 2017, I’ve helped make email more trustworthy with Valimail. Valimail stops phishing emails automatically. It helps businesses protect their customers and employees from fraud.

Since 2006, I’ve used sports to bring people together with PeacePlayers, DUPLAYS, and Playpass. Playpass makes life easier for managers of sports leagues with an online league scheduler. It also provides software for online payments, referrals, waivers, and a website builder.

In 2013, I was named a “high-impact entrepreneur” by Endeavor Global.

I also played basketball at Harvard and as a pro in 4 countries.
